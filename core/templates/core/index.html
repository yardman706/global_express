Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Built for businesses that need speed, visibility, and peace of mind at every step of the journey.

How do I track my shipment?

Enter your tracking number on our Track Shipment page for detailed status updates including estimated delivery time and proof of delivery.

What areas do you deliver to?

We offer domestic delivery nationwide and international shipping to over 50 countries through our global partner network. Contact us for specific route availability and transit times.

How are shipping rates calculated?

Rates depend on package weight, dimensions, destination, and service level (express, standard, or economy). Request a free quote online or speak with our team for volume pricing on regular shipments.

Do you offer insurance for shipments?

Yes. All shipments include basic coverage at no extra cost. Additional insurance is available for high-value, fragile, or specialty items, ask your account manager when booking.

What is the cutoff time for same-day pickup?

Book by 2:00 PM local time for same-day pickup in most metropolitan areas. Contact our team to confirm availability in your region or to arrange scheduled recurring pickups.
